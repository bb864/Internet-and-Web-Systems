G Vishwanath
Posted on:

Well, my name is G Vishwanath and I used to be a famous cricketer during the nineteen sixties and seventies when we played cricket with tennis balls in the streets of Mumbai where I grew up. But my fame did not spread beyond the streets in the neighbourhood I lived in.
Later, another clever and talented cricketer usurped my name, became even more famous than I was and started playing test matches and scored a century on his first test appearance, and got to be known as one of Indias most stylish and talented batsmen.
Sigh! I wish I had copyrighted my name.
It poor comfort to me to know that we were both born in 1949 and are of the same height and live in the same city.
